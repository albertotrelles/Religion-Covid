\underline{Model}

$$y_{ic} = \alpha_c + \lambda_i +\gamma (year \times T_{ic}) + \beta x_{i-1,c} $$
$$y_{ic} = \alpha_c + \lambda_i +\gamma (year \times T_{ic}) + \delta T_{ic} + \beta x_{i-1,c} $$

\begin{itemize}
    \item $T$: dummy $=1$ since lockdown in 2020. Also $=1$ for 2019 paired observations. 
    \item $year$: 2020 dummy.
    \item $x$: daily covid-19 controls at the state level (deaths, averaged deaths, cases, averaged cases). 
    \item $\alpha_c$: country/state fixed effects. 
    \item $\lambda_i$: time fixed effects - year (2019, 2020), week of year (1-15), day of week (Monday-Sunday). 
    \item SE clustered at the day of year level (100 clusters, 2 observations per cluster).
\end{itemize}

Obs. $year \times T$ is equivalent to the post-treatment variable in the standard TWFE setup.
